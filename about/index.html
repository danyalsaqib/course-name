About

I’m an MSc student at the University of Calgary’s Department of Mechanical and Manufacturing Engineering, where I do research focusing on the development of Compliant Control methods for Human-Robot Interaction, via adaptive control policies and learning algorithms. My supervisor is Dr. Marie Charbonneau.

I previously completed my Bachelor’s degree in Electrical Engineering from the School of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science (SEECS) at the National University of Sciences and Technology (NUST), Islamabad, Pakistan. My Bachelor’s Thesis focused on a self-supervised robotic grasping setup. This project was supervised by Dr. Wajahat Hussain.

In my free time, I do try to go out for walks and try new food (though I often end up in front of my monitor). I find graphics design incredibly therapeutic! My portfolio can be found here.
